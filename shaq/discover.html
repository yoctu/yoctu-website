About ShaQ

Our bidding engine can be integrated seamlessly to any existing environment. ShaQ will fit all types of ERP (Oracle, SAP, In-House..). If you are a Shipper, a TMS, or any actor of the supply chain, it will be your next generation tool to get your quotes together. As the freight industry is facing its own digital revolution, you don't want to fall behind, let's ShaQ !

Product info

Invitation Rules:

You can create a public or a private ShaQ which means you can either choose the allowed bidders or open your quotation to all the bidders on the platform.

Plant:
Define the ACL of your organisation.

Get it now:
Set up your targets and let the system do the rest via the auto-acception of the bids fitting the objectives.

Bidders management:
Enable or Disable the Bidders at will.

Decision Time:
No validation of a bid can made before the time set is reached.

Bid file upload:
Upload a file joined to any of your bids.

Shaq validity:
Your ShaQ can be open as long as you choose.

Bid validity:
Once you bid on a ShaQ, you can set up the expiration time of your offer.

Custom Settings:
Define your currency, any system of measurement, and many more.

Chat:
Live chat in the Shaq with the ones you want!

Notifications:
You can decide which way you want to receive the updates about what’s going on: mail,discord,rss, web notification, etc…

Raters:
Our Rating Engines option allow you to compare any of your negotiated rates with the ongoing bids of your quotation.

Ranking/Rating:
You can rate and rank your bids and bidders.

Custom design:
Use your corporate logo and colours thanks to our custom branding of the ShaQ.

Authentication:
Saml with Oauth provider (google, facebook...).

Open Shaq:
APIs and Websocket for your applications.
